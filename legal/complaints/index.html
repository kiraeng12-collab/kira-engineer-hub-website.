How to submit a complaint

Send your full name, email, Telegram username, product, payment reference if relevant, complaint description, evidence and requested resolution to info@kiraengineer.com.

Acknowledgement and investigation

Complaints should be acknowledged and investigated according to a response time chosen by the owner: [COMPLAINT RESPONSE TIME TO BE CONFIRMED].

Complaint types

Complaints may relate to payment issues, community conduct, membership access, privacy matters, content concerns or technical access.

Records, confidentiality and escalation

Complaint records should be kept confidential and not exposed publicly. External rights under applicable law are not limited. Do not claim ombudsman or regulator access unless verified.
