Data may be used to respond to inquiries, provide access, process payments through payment providers, operate communities, handle complaints, manage privacy requests, secure the service and improve content.

Payment details are generally processed by payment providers where applicable; Kira should not store full card details. Telegram, social-media platforms, hosting providers, technical providers and email systems may process data under their own terms.

Retention, security and transfers

Data is retained only as needed for the relevant purpose and legal/accounting requirements once configured. International transfers may occur through third-party platforms. Reasonable security measures should be maintained.

Privacy rights

Depending on where you live and which laws apply, you may have certain privacy rights, including access, correction, deletion, objection, consent withdrawal or marketing opt-out.

Children, updates and contact
