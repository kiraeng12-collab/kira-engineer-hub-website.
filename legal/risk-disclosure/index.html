Risk Disclosure

Last updated: July 2026 - Status: Active Draft

Prominent risk warning Trading financial markets carries substantial risk and may result in partial or total loss of capital.

Leveraged products can produce rapid losses. Past performance does not guarantee future performance. Market commentary, technical analysis, signals, scenarios and discussions are educational and can be wrong. Technical analysis is not certainty.

Users are responsible for their own trading decisions and should not trade money they cannot afford to lose. Users should evaluate whether trading is appropriate for their knowledge, financial position and risk tolerance, and may need independent professional advice.

This disclosure is not a tool to excuse misleading advertising. Kira Engineer Hub should communicate risk clearly wherever products, membership, commentary or future tools are presented.
