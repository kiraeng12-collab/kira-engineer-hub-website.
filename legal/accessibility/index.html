Accessibility Statement

Last updated: July 2026 - Status: Active Draft

We aim to align the website with recognised accessibility practices and continue improving areas that may create barriers.

Accessibility goals

The site is designed for keyboard navigation, visible focus states, screen-reader-compatible structure, readable contrast, responsive layouts and comfortable text sizing.

Known limitations

Third-party platforms such as Telegram, Instagram, email clients and payment providers are outside direct control. Perfect WCAG compliance is not claimed without formal testing.

Report a problem

Email info@kiraengineer.com with the page URL, device, browser and issue description.
