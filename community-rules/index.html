Community Rules

Respectful communication

Members must communicate respectfully. Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, intimidation and abusive language are not allowed.

Language and relevance

Keep discussion clear, relevant and understandable for the community. If English is required in a specific space, moderators may ask members to use English.

No spam or unauthorised promotion

No spam, unsolicited promotions, unauthorised affiliate links, broker promotion without approval, fake engagement, impersonation or financial solicitation is allowed.

Private content protection

Private membership content, analysis, screenshots, signals, scenarios and resources must not be reposted, resold, recorded or redistributed.

No misleading claims

Do not post false performance claims, guaranteed-profit claims, manipulated screenshots, fake results or claims that Kira manages funds.

Security and funds

Do not request or accept funds on behalf of Kira unless through an official approved channel. Do not share passwords, API keys or private account data.

Moderation

Moderators may warn, restrict, suspend or remove members to protect the community. Misconduct can be reported to support.
