KIRA Partner Program

Partner with a risk-aware trading education brand.

Approved partners may refer suitable audiences to Kira Engineer Hub while following clear promotion, disclosure and compliance rules.

What the program is

The KIRA Partner Program is an invite-only or manually approved referral program for creators, educators and community partners whose promotion style fits the brand.

Who may apply

Applicants should have a relevant audience, a responsible communication style, and willingness to avoid misleading financial claims.

How referrals work

Referral attribution is expected to use approved links or codes. The referral window is 30 days.

Commission model

Initial commission is 20% of eligible collected membership revenue during the referred customer's first 90 days. Payouts are monthly after a 30-day validation period, with a USD 50 minimum payout.

Validation and payment

Eligible sales require a new customer, successful payment, valid attribution, no unresolved refund, fraud or chargeback, no self-referral and no existing-member reassignment.

Promotion rules

Partners must not guarantee profits, imply risk-free trading, fabricate results, use fake screenshots, impersonate Kira Engineer, spam users, or promote unauthorized discounts.

Affiliate disclosure example: "Affiliate disclosure: I may earn a commission if you join through my link."

FAQ

Are all applicants accepted?

No. The program is invite-only or manually approved.

Can I use paid ads?

Only if approved and compliant with program rules. Brand-keyword bidding and misleading ads are restricted.

Can I promise trading results?

No. Affiliates must not promise profits, low-risk outcomes or guaranteed performance.
