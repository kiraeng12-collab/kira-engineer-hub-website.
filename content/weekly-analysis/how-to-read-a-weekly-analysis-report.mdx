This post explains the format future KIRA Weekly Analysis reports will follow.
It is not a market report itself, and does not reference any specific
instrument, date, or price level.

## Report structure

Every published report will follow the same four sections:

1. **Macro overview** - the broader context and scheduled events relevant to
   the instruments covered that week.
2. **Bullish and bearish scenarios** - both directions are described side by
   side, since the point is to prepare for either outcome rather than commit
   to one.
3. **Key levels and risk considerations** - structural levels worth being
   aware of, framed as context rather than instructions.
4. **Educational disclaimer** - repeated on every report, without exception.

## What a report is not

A Weekly Analysis report is not a signal service, a personal recommendation,
or a guarantee of any market outcome. It exists to show the reasoning process
behind a market view, so members can build their own - not to replace it.
