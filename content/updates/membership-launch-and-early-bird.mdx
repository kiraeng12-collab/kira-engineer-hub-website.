KIRA VIP Membership is scheduled to open on 1 August 2026. This update covers
what that means for pricing and for members who joined official Kira spaces
before the cutoff.

## Standard pricing

- **KIRA VIP Monthly**: USD 70 per month.
- **KIRA VIP Quarterly**: USD 189 every three months.

## Early Bird eligibility

Members who joined qualifying official Kira spaces before 1 August 2026 at
12:00 AM Gulf Standard Time may be eligible for a 20% Early Bird discount on
either plan. Eligibility is verified, not automatic - see the
[Early Bird Lifetime Discount Eligibility](/early-bird) page for the
verification process and full terms.

## What doesn't change

Free community access through Telegram remains free. Membership is optional
and adds structured content and access on top of it - it does not gate
participation in the free community.
